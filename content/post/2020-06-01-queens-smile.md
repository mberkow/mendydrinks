There isn't much to smile about in the world today. The sweetness of the Drambuie and Apricot are not enough to overpower the botanicals and lemon in this drink.

* 1 1/2 oz Gin
* 1/2 oz Apricot Liquor
* 1/2 oz Lemon Juice
* 1/2 oz Drambuie
