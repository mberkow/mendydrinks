It never ceases to amaze me how potent peaty scotch can be. I think this needed a little more strawberry. 

{{< figure src="/images/2020-05-06-straw-dog.jpg" >}}

* 1 Strawberry
* 1 1/4 oz Scotch
* 1/4 oz Peated Scotch
* 1 oz Dry Vermouth
* 3/4 oz Lemon Juice
* 1/2 oz Simple Syrup
* 1 Dash bitters

Via [Death & Co](https://www.deathandcompany.com/)
