A nice refreshing tiki drink after a blazing hot day. The pineapple shines through this drink and the orgeat gets lost in the background. 

{{< figure src="/images/2020-07-26-beachbum.jpg" >}}

* 2 oz Rum
* 1 oz Pineapple Juice
* 3/4 oz Lime Juice
* 1/2 Apricot Liquor
* 1/2 oz Orgeat
